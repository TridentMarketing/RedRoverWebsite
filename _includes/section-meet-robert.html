Meet Robert Bouse

Campground Consulting in Redefining the Outdoor Experience

Red Rover Camping is guided by the experience and leadership of Robert Bouse, a longtime leader in the outdoor hospitality industry with more than 40 years of campground and resort management experience.

Working alongside resort operations teams, Robert helps oversee the management of Red Rover Camping properties, focusing on delivering exceptional guest service, well-maintained resorts, and memorable outdoor experiences for campers and RV travelers. His hands-on approach ensures that each resort operates at the highest standards while creating welcoming destinations where families can relax, explore, and enjoy the camping lifestyle.

Robert's decades of experience with leading organizations across the campground industry help shape the Red Rover vision: great resorts, great service, and unforgettable camping experiences.
