About the Resort

Your Black Hills Adventure Home

23680 Busted Five Court, Rapid City, South Dakota 57702

At Rushmore Shadows Resort, adventure and relaxation go hand in hand. From our heated pool and mini golf course to scenic walking trails and community fire pits, there's something here for everyone to enjoy. Located just minutes from Mount Rushmore and the best of the Black Hills, this resort is the perfect base for your South Dakota adventure.

Explore the Area

Nearby Attractions

The Black Hills region offers world-class attractions just minutes from the resort.

Mount Rushmore

Marvel at the iconic national memorial featuring the carved faces of four U.S. presidents.

Crazy Horse Memorial

A stunning tribute to the Lakota leader with a museum of Native American history and culture.

Custer State Park

Stunning landscapes, diverse wildlife, hiking, and scenic drives — a must-see destination.

Bear Country USA

Drive through to see bears, elk, bison, and wildlife up close. Don't miss the baby animals!

Reptile Gardens

Exotic reptiles, birds, live shows, and interactive exhibits — fun and educational for all ages.

Rush Mountain Adventure Park

Mountain coaster, cave tours, and gemstone mining — thrilling fun for the whole family.
