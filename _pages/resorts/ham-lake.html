About the Resort

Your Twin Cities Lakefront Escape

2400 Constance Blvd NE, Ham Lake, Minnesota 55304

At Ham Lake Resort, adventure and relaxation go hand in hand. From boating and swimming on the lake to scenic trails and a lively activity pavilion, there is something here for everyone to enjoy. Conveniently located just north of the Twin Cities metro, this lakefront gem offers easy access from Minneapolis and St. Paul — the perfect seasonal escape without a long drive.
