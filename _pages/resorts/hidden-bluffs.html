About the Resort

Your Bluff Country Nature Escape

22885 County Road 19, Spring Grove, Minnesota 55974

At Hidden Bluffs Resort, nature and relaxation go hand in hand. From fishing in the trout stream and hiking wooded trails to cooling off in the pool and challenging the family to mini golf, there is something here for everyone to enjoy. Nestled in Minnesota's scenic bluff country, this tranquil retreat offers the perfect escape from everyday life — approximately 2 hours from Minneapolis.
