About the Resort

Your Midwest Adventure Home

74978 Broadhead Road, Kimbolton, Ohio 43749

At Rocky Fork Ranch, adventure and relaxation go hand in hand. From scenic horseback rides and hiking trails to indoor fun like karaoke and game rooms, there's something here for everyone to enjoy. Open year-round, this Ohio ranch resort offers an unmatched variety of activities for families and adventure seekers alike.

Nearby Attractions

Discover all that eastern Ohio has to offer just a short drive from Rocky Fork Ranch.

National Museum of Cambridge Glass

Explore exquisite glass pieces and learn about the craftsmanship that defined an era.

Salt Fork Marina

Boating, fishing, and water sports on Ohio's largest inland beach.

Roscoe Village

A restored 19th-century canal town with shops, tours, and live demonstrations.

The Wilds

Safari tours, zip-lining, and rare wildlife at this unique conservation center.

John and Annie Glenn Museum

Visit astronaut John Glenn's childhood home with interactive exhibits.

Great American Steam Locomotive Museum

Impressive steam locomotives and railroad memorabilia at this fascinating museum.
