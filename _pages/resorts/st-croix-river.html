About the Resort

Your Ultimate River Adventure Home

40756 Grace Lake Road, Hinckley, Minnesota 55037

From canoeing and kayaking on the St. Croix River to exploring miles of ATV trails and fishing for walleye, St. Croix River Resort offers endless outdoor adventures for the whole family. Tucked into a beautiful pine forest along Minnesota's scenic St. Croix River corridor, this private resort is a true hidden gem for outdoor enthusiasts looking for an unforgettable escape.

Nearby Attractions

Explore the natural beauty and adventures of the St. Croix River region.

Gandy Dancer ATV Trails

30-mile trail along an abandoned railroad grade starting in Danbury, WI through scenic Minnesota landscapes.

St. Croix State Park

Minnesota's largest state park — over 34,000 acres of natural beauty and outdoor activities.

Grand Casino Hinckley

Entertainment, dining, and gaming excitement at Grand Casino Hinckley.

Hinckley Fire Museum

Learn about the devastating Great Hinckley Fire of 1894 at this engaging museum.

St. Croix State Forest ATV Trails

26 miles of scenic trails along the Wisconsin border through heavily wooded forests.

Pardon Canoe & Tube Rental

Just 5 miles away in Danbury, WI — a fun day of canoeing and tubing on the scenic river.
