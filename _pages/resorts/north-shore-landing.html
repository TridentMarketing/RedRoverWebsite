About the Resort

Your Lake Oconee Getaway

2541 Carey Station Road, Greensboro, Georgia 30642

Nestled on 55 beautiful acres along Lake Oconee near Atlanta, North Shore Landing Resort combines a one-of-a-kind railroad theme with premier lakefront amenities. Authentic boxcars, club cars, and cabooses create an experience unlike any other — the perfect home base for families, adventurers, and outdoor enthusiasts looking for an unforgettable camping experience.
